# B2B EdTech Lead Analytics Project

End-to-End Data Analytics Project using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to analyze B2B EdTech lead generation, engagement, acquisition channels, customer profiles, and conversion performance.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze the complete lead lifecycle and identify key factors affecting lead generation, engagement, and conversion performance.

The project focuses on:

- Lead Acquisition Analysis
- Marketing Performance Analysis
- Lead Engagement Analysis
- Customer Profile Analysis
- Conversion Performance Analysis
- Business Insights & Recommendations

## Key Business Insights

- High-relevance leads achieved the highest conversion rates.
- Google emerged as the top-performing marketing source.
- Better Career Prospects was the most common enrollment goal.
- Higher engagement levels were associated with stronger conversion outcomes.
- Mumbai contributed the highest lead volume.
- Lead quality significantly impacted conversion performance.

---

## Recommendations

- Focus marketing spend on high-converting channels.
- Improve lead qualification and scoring processes.
- Increase engagement among medium and low-engagement prospects.
- Expand campaigns in top-performing cities.
- Continuously monitor lead quality and conversion metrics.
